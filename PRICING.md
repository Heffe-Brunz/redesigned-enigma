## 1. What counts as one question

A single inquiry plus **up to two follow-up clarifications on the same matter** = 1 question.

A new matter, a different employee, or a different legislative provision = a new question.

This prevents a single chatty thread from draining the Essential allowance.

---

## 2. SLA hours

**Business hours: Monday – Friday, 07:00–17:00 CAT.**

Questions received outside these hours are queued; the SLA clock starts at 07:00 the next business day.

### Emergency escalation (Professional & Site only)

For night-shift incidents (e.g. Mines Act s.188 occurrence at 23:00), Professional and Site subscribers may trigger an emergency escalation via a dedicated WhatsApp channel. Response target is **2 hours**, measured from the escalation message. Escalations are limited to **2 per calendar month**; additional escalations are billed at NAD 750 each.

Mining clients on Essential who need out-of-hours cover should upgrade to Professional or Site.

---

## 3. Fair-use policy (replaces "unlimited")

Professional and Site questions are capped at **40 per month per subscription** to protect SLA integrity during heavy periods (e.g. mass retrenchment cycles).

**Overage:** NAD 150 per question, invoiced at month-end.

If a subscriber consistently exceeds 40 questions for three consecutive months, we will proactively discuss whether a Site or multi-seat arrangement better fits their needs.

---

## 4. Scope

### In-scope

- Labour Act 11 of 2007 — interpretation, procedural guidance, precedent references
- Mines Act — section 188 incident classification and reporting obligations
- Occupational Safety & Health Act — section 42 interpretation
- Employment code and workplace policy review (document-level)
- Procedural guidance for disciplinary, grievance, and retrenchment processes

### Out-of-scope

LabourAssist works **alongside legal counsel** and does not replace it. The following are explicitly excluded:

- Court representation or preparation of pleadings
- Definitive legal opinions (our guidance is advisory, not a legal opinion for evidentiary purposes)
- Conducting disciplinary or arbitration hearings on the client's behalf
- Advice on matters governed by legislation not listed above without prior written agreement

Clients requiring out-of-scope services will be referred to a panel of qualified labour lawyers.

## 5. Platform upgrade path

Every **Professional** and **Site** subscriber receives:

> **50 % off the LabourAssist platform for the first 6 months** from their activation date, once the platform launches.

This is confirmed in the subscriber onboarding message and recorded against the account. No action is required — the discount is applied automatically at sign-up.

Site subscribers additionally receive **priority queue access** during the platform beta.

The hotline is the funnel. The platform is the destination.

## 6. Tier selection guide

| Subscriber profile | Recommended tier |
|---|---|
| Single HR manager, ≤ 10 matters/month, statutory minimum cover | Essential |
| HR manager or compliance officer with steady caseload, needs SLA reliability | Professional |
| Multi-site mining or smelting group, 2–3 HR/legal staff sharing a subscription | Site |

_Examples: Rössing Uranium, Namibia Custom Smelters, Weatherly Mining Namibia → Site_

---

## 7. Billing & cancellation

- Invoices are issued on the 1st of each month, payable within 14 days.
- Cancellation requires 30 days' written notice; no partial-month refunds.
- Overage charges appear on the following month's invoice with an itemised question log.
- Tier changes (upgrade or downgrade) take effect on the next billing cycle.
